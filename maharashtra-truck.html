Maharashtra Truck Project

Vehicle Simulation Unreal Engine Blueprints

A detailed virtual simulation of the Maharashtra truck transportation model, focusing on realistic road environments, vehicle behavior, and operational logistics.

Client: Transport Authority of Maharashtra
Software: Unreal Engine
Type: Simulation
Goal: Create an interactive and visually accurate simulation for training and visualization purposes.

My role

Modeled vehicle assets, implemented driving physics, developed environment and road network, and scripted interactive scenarios.

Tools / Stack

Unreal Engine, Blueprints, Material Editor

Context

Simulation project for optimizing road transport visualization.

Process

  • Design truck and environmental assets with realistic textures.
  • Set up driving mechanics and physics using Unreal Engine Blueprints.
  • Integrate camera systems for multiple viewing modes.
  • Test and refine simulation to ensure accuracy and performance.
